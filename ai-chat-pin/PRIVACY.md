# Privacy Policy — AI Chat Pin

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

AI Chat Pin is a Chrome extension that allows users to pin important messages from AI chat services (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini) for note-taking and session handoff purposes.

## Data Collection

**AI Chat Pin does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data created by this extension — including pinned messages, notes, and handoff text — is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) on your own device.

## What the Extension Accesses

| Access | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Page content on claude.ai, chatgpt.com, gemini.google.com | To display pin buttons next to chat messages |
| Browser local storage | To save and retrieve your pinned messages and settings |
| Active tab information | To identify which AI service you are currently using |

## What the Extension Does NOT Do

- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not track browsing activity
- Does not use analytics or advertising services
- Does not access pages outside of claude.ai, chatgpt.com, and gemini.google.com
- Does not require account creation or login

## Data Storage and Deletion

All pinned messages, notes, and handoff text are stored locally in your browser. You can delete all stored data at any time using the "Clear All" button in the extension popup.

Uninstalling the extension will remove all associated local data.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
