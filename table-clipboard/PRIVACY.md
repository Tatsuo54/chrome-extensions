# Privacy Policy — Table Clipboard

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

Table Clipboard is a Chrome extension that allows users to effortlessly collect table data from any webpage into a side panel with a single click, and export them later in CSV, TXT, or Markdown formats.

## Data Collection

**Table Clipboard does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data created and extracted by this extension — including the saved table contents, source URLs, and page titles — is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) on your own device.

## What the Extension Accesses

| Access | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Browser local storage | To save and retrieve your collected tables and language preferences. |
| Page content on all websites (`<all_urls>`) | To inject a content script that detects `<table>` tags on the webpage you are browsing, highlights them, and extracts the text data when you click on a table. |
| Side Panel | To display the collected tables and provide export/delete functionalities alongside your browsing experience. |

## What the Extension Does NOT Do

- Does not send any data to external servers or cloud databases
- Does not track your browsing activity or history
- Does not use analytics or advertising services
- Does not require account creation or login
- Does not collect personally identifiable information

## Data Storage and Deletion

Collected tables are stored locally in your browser (up to 30 tables). You can delete individual tables or clear all tables at any time using the "Delete" or "Delete all" buttons in the side panel.

Uninstalling the extension will instantly remove all associated local data.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
